SnipCode

Smart coupon assistant for online shoppers

Automatic discount codes at checkout

SnipCode is a Chrome extension that helps shoppers save money by automatically searching, testing, and suggesting valid discount codes at the checkout step on e-commerce websites. Users engage with SnipCode at the most valuable moment: right before confirming their purchase.

Value for brands & advertisers

SnipCode connects high-intent shoppers with partner brands at the point of purchase. By surfacing verified offers and affiliate links, we help increase conversions while keeping the user experience clean and compliant.

High-intent traffic Shoppers interact with SnipCode during checkout when they have already decided to buy.
Verified promo codes We surface trusted, up-to-date coupon codes and offers from partnered brands.
Clean user experience Lightweight extension, no spammy pop-ups, and fully aligned with browser policies.

Promotion methods

We promote partner brands through:

Automatic coupon suggestions SnipCode suggests the best available discount code directly in the checkout flow.
In-extension placements Partner stores and offers are highlighted inside the extension UI.
Affiliate link redirection Purchases are tracked via affiliate links provided by partner networks.
